A hamburger is a sandwich consisting of a cooked, ground meat patty—typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll

A hot dog is a fully cooked, cured, and often smoked sausage—typically made from beef, pork, chicken, or turkey—served hot in a partially sliced bun

An item of food consisting of two pieces of bread with meat, cheese, or other filling between them, eaten as a light meal.
